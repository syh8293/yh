The "Errors" in the King James Bible

In this book, Dr. Ruckman examines four hundred so-called contradictions, which scholars have supposedly found in the King James Bible, and he answers every major attack on the words of the King James text as we presently have it. He shows that no one has ever proven one error in that text. The reader is given complete, irrefutable, and Biblical answers to the “corrections” made by the scholars in these alleged “problem texts.” An indispensable tool for answering the Bible’s critics; one ministry has even used this book as a resource for successfully countering the Moslems’ attacks on the Bible and answering those Moslems who are sincerely seeking the truth about the Bible and Jesus Christ. (Print Length: 511 pages)
